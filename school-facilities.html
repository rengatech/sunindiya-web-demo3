SCHOOL FACILITIES

LIBRARY

The library has various books covering various topics and genres to cater to diverse interests.

School Bus

School buses are provided to transport students to school as well as for field trips and other school-related events

Play Ground

The school has a huge playground with a volleyball and basketball court. It has both indoor as well as outdoor games.

Drawing class

One of the essential facilities in a school drawing class is a spacious and well-lit classroom.

Canteen

The school has distinct and separate science laboratories for practical sessions in Physics, Chemistry, Home Science, and Biology per the CBSE curriculum.

Lab

The school has distinct and separate science laboratories for practical sessions in Physics, Chemistry, Home Science, and Biology in accordance with the CBSE curriculum.

Food Menu

Breakfast, lunch, and departure snacks are provided every day to children. The regular food items cooked in the canteen are meals with fish/meat, Idly sambar, Thosa chutney, Tea, and Coffee.
