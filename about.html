About us

Education is imparting knowledge that helps a child to gain confidence and self-respect. Education should enable the child to stand on his own feet and develop his morality, humanity, honesty and character health apart from academic excellence.

SunIndiya Public School. believes in a comprehensive system of education that will help to develop children into well-groomed personalities and channelize their inherent talents. We are committed to provide a system of instruction that will encourage the thought process and reasoning, which would gear children to be logical, analytical, practical, creative and innovative.

    The school is housed in a modern well-furnished, elegant building situated in a peaceful setting in a sprawling landscape with essential facilities like spacious digital classrooms, laboratories, library, playground and garden.

    The School has a team of highly qualified teaching staff with vast experience. Special efforts are made to give personal attention to each and every student of the school so as to help each one to achieve his/her full potential.

    25 + trainers

    Apart from long duration induction training courses for newly recruited teaching staff, short duration in-service courses like orientation courses, seminars, workshops etc. are also organized as a part of teachers training programme every year.

  • directions_bus

    Available School Bus

    The school employs trained and licensed bus drivers responsible for transporting students safely and efficiently.The school establishes safe and efficient bus routes taking into account factors such as traffic, weather and student demographics. The school has designated safe and convenient bus stops for students.

    Play Ground

    The school has a huge playground with a volleyball and basketball court. It has both indoor as well as outdoor games. Special coaching is given for the talented students in the field of sports to shine at regional, state and national level sports competitions by our trained and experienced physical education trainers and coaches.

    Canteen

    Our school Canteen serves healthy, nutritious and fresh food throughout the year to students. Breakfast, lunch and departure snacks are provided every day to children. A subsidized canteen fully air conditioned is functioning within the premises of the Institute to serve hygienically prepared food
