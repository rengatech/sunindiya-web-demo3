About us

Education is imparting knowledge that helps a child to gain confidence and self-respect. Education should enable the child to stand on his own feet and develop his morality, humanity, honesty, and character health apart from academic excellence.

Sunindiya Public School. believes in a comprehensive education system that will help develop children into well-groomed personalities and channel their inherent talents. We are committed to providing a method of instruction that will encourage the thought process and reasoning, which would gear children to be logical, analytical, practical, creative, and innovative.

SCHOOL FACILITIES

Library

The library has a wide range of books covering a wide range of topics and genres to cater to the diverse interests .

School Bus

School buses are provided to transport students to school as well as for field trips and other school related events.

Play Ground

The school has a huge playground with a volleyball and basketball court. It has both indoor as well as outdoor games.

Drawing class

One of the essential facilities in a school drawing class is a spacious and well-lit classroom.

Canteen

Our school Canteen serves healthy, nutritious, and fresh food throughout the year for students.

Lab

The school has distinct and separate science laboratories for practical sessions in Physics, Chemistry,Science, and Biology per the CBSE curriculum

Grand Open

Welcome everyone to the grand opening of our school! We are thrilled to have you all here to celebrate this special occasion with us.

Today marks a significant milestone in our school's history, as we officially open our doors to students, parents, and the wider community. We are excited to showcase our state-of-the-art facilities, talented staff, and innovative curriculum.

Our school's mission is to provide an exceptional education that prepares students for success in the 21st century. We believe in fostering a love of learning and encouraging our students to become critical thinkers, problem solvers, and global citizens.

We would like to extend our heartfelt thanks to all the parents, teachers, and staff who have worked tirelessly to make this grand opening possible. We are truly grateful for your dedication and commitment to our school's vision.

As we embark on this new journey together, we look forward to building strong relationships with our students, parents, and community partners. We are confident that our school will be a place where students thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.
